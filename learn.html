Community & Contributions

Become a Community Member

By registering you will have the opportunity to upload and download from our community-driven sound effects library. Members can share their assets by making them available for purchase with our in-app "coin" currency.

Coins can be used to buy sounds that are available for download, as well as whole SFX packs curated and designed by SF Audio Guild.

You can earn coins when other members choose to download your uploaded tracks! You can also earn coins by becoming a contributor for SF Audio Guild.

Become a Contributor

Contributors will have the opportunity to prove themselves as "master-sound-designers" by uploading hand-crafted audio assets to our open projects. These will include a variety of entries based on a common theme. Members may contribute up to the total amount expected for any entry on a project.

When a contribution has been approved, the contributor will receive in-app coins as compensation for their hard-work. We will be using this interaction to vet potential new in-house "Guild Members" for our company.

The more you make the better the chances we will contact you for contract work.

What does it take to be a contributor?

- You will need to sign our contributor-agreement and provide some basic personal information.
- You will need to have basic proficiency with audio equipment and Digital Audio Workstations.
